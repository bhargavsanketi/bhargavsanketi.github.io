I am currently a senior scientist at Altos labs at their San Diego institute of science, advised by Dr. Juan Carlos Belmonte . I was previously a Ph.D. candidate in Dr. Natasza Kurpios' lab and the field of biochemistry, molecular and cell biology at Cornell university, New York. I previously earned an undergraduate degree in Biotechnology from RV College of Engineering, India.

I am facinated by the systems-level accuracy and precision with which organisms are engineered. In my current work, I am trying to understand how aging and fibrosis affects tissue homeostasis and exploring cellular reprogramming as an approach to reverse these decline. Previously, I studied how the intestine obtains its characteristic structures and functions during embryonic development and adult homeostasis.
