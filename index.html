Bhargav D. Sanketi

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Dr. Natasza Kurpios' lab and the field of biochemistry, molecular and cell biology at Cornell university. I previously earned an undergraduate degree in Biotechnology from RV College of Engineering, India.

I am facinated by the systems-level accuracy and precision with which organisms are engineered. In my current work, I am trying to understand how the intestine obtains its characteristic structures and functions during embryonic development and adult homeostasis. I use a wide range of techniques spanning single-cell transcriptomics, mouse genetics and physiology, modern approaches to chicken embryology, high-resolution/ 3D imaging, measurement and manipulation of gene expression, cell signaling and tissue mechanics.
